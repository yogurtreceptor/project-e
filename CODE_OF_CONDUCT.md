# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

Project E contributors, maintainers and community members commit to making participation welcoming and free from harassment, regardless of background, identity, experience or ability.

## Expected behaviour

- Be respectful, constructive and patient.
- Critique ideas and changes, not people.
- Welcome questions and different levels of experience.
- Respect privacy. Do not share another person's personal information without permission.
- Accept responsibility, apologise when appropriate and learn from mistakes.

## Unacceptable behaviour

Harassment, discrimination, threats, sexualised attention, deliberate intimidation, sustained disruption, personal attacks and publishing private information without permission are not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove contributions and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation when behaviour is inappropriate, threatening, offensive or harmful.

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository owner through their GitHub profile. Do not include sensitive details in a public issue. Reports will be reviewed as promptly and impartially as practical, and information will be shared only as needed to investigate and respond.

## Scope

This policy applies in Project E repositories and community spaces, and when someone is officially representing the project elsewhere.
